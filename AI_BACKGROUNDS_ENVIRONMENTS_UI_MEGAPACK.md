## üé® STYLE CONSISTENCY TIPS

### Maintaining Visual Cohesion

1. **Pick a style and stick to it**
   - Choose pixel art density (16-bit SNES, 32-bit, etc.)
   - Maintain color palette consistency
   - Use same line thickness

2. **Color Harmony**
   - Use complementary colors
   - Limit palette per asset
   - Consistent saturation levels

3. **Detail Level**
   - Match complexity across assets
   - Same level of polish
   - Consistent anti-aliasing

4. **Lighting Consistency**
   - Same light direction across related assets
   - Consistent shadow intensity
   - Unified glow effects
